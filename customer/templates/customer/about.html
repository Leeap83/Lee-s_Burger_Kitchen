Our Story

Who is LBK?

Lee's Burger Kitchen or LBK as we are commonly known is named after the founder/head chef who started making homemade Burgers in his kitchen. LBK first started as a pop up business at markets, festivals local events. Eventually as our reputation grew so did demand, and we eventually opened our first LBK in the heart of Shrewsbury Town Centre.

What makes us different?

Easy it's the taste!
Freshly made beef patties + locally sourced produce = one tasty burger. We like to experiment with flavours and create a burger that not only we enjoy but hopefully so do our customers. We also encourage our customers to create their own burger and if we like it we'll add it to our menu.

Do you Deliver?

We deliver to all address in the following postcode areas SY1, SY2, SY3, SY4, SY5, SY6. We charge 5% of your order total for delivery costs. We also offer a collection services, please allow 15 mins from when your order is being cooked before you arrive to collect.
